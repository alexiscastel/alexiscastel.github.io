Critical Success Factors – DO NOT COMPROMISE

  • Calf Strain Recovery: Pain MUST remain <1/10 on easy runs, <2/10 on threshold/long efforts. If pain escalates, revert immediately to Week 1–2 protocol (easy runs only, no threshold/long-run until day 14+ post-incident).

  • Threshold Sessions as Decision Points (Apr 8 + Apr 14): If either triggers calf pain, cancel remaining intensity and extend recovery week immediately.
  • Cycling Integration Must Be Tracked: 2–3 sessions/week (60–90 min Z2) are PRIMARY aerobic base builders. Without load system tracking, ACWR calculations skew running-heavy. Use TrainingPeaks, Whoop, or coach log.
  • Recovery Days Enforcement: Move from current 1 per 10–12 days → 1–2 per 7 days. Monday or Friday easy sessions can become zero-load days if cumulative fatigue evident (TSB <−5, RHR up >1 bpm, subjective fatigue).
  • Dirty Dash HM Taper (Apr 16–18): Thu–Fri must be 48 hr ZLD + mobility only. Acute load should dip to 50–60 EWMA by race day to ensure readiness.
  • Post-Phase 1 Assessment (Apr 19–20): If calf pain-free AND Dirty Dash HM executed well, Phase 2 proceeds to full build (hill repeats, 2–2.5 hr Z2, cycling 90–120 min). If calf pain persists OR race poor quality, extend Phase 1 recovery (Apr 20–May 3) before Phase 2 ramp.

Running Zones

  • Z1 (Easy): ≤139 bpm (~10:00–11:00 min/mi) | Recovery, aerobic base
  • Z2 (Endurance): 140–159 bpm (~8:30–9:45 min/mi) | Long-runs, cycling base
  • Z3 (Tempo): 160–174 bpm (~7:45–8:30 min/mi) | Sustained effort, threshold
  • Z4 (Threshold): 175–185 bpm (~6:30–7:30 min/mi) | VO₂ work, race pacing
  • Z5 (VO₂max): >186 bpm (<6:30 min/mi) | High-intensity repeats
