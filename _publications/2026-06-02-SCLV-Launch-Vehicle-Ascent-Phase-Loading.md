This presentation compares traditional and CFD-based approaches for estimating launch vehicle ascent phase loading. The material reviews how aerodynamic pressure environments can be characterized for vibro-acoustic and structural-dynamics analyses, and discusses the modeling trade-offs involved when moving from empirical or semi-empirical methods toward CFD-informed loading definitions.
